Introduction to Rotaries and the Mazda RX-8!

Welcome to my (Richard Gaal's) website! Its dedicated to rotary engines and the Mazda RX-8. My goal is for this to be a "one stop shop" for information about the RX-8. Things I plan to host here include:

  • A brief description of rotar engines and how they compare to normal piston engines.
  • The rotary-specific things you have to keep in mind to keep the engine healthy
  • How to perform basic maintenance and some (easyish) longevity modifications
